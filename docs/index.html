How it works

Three steps to your first card.

1
w.roll
Type the command

Drop w.roll in any channel where WikiRoll is active.

›
2
Pick a card
Pick from 10 characters

The bot posts 10 cards from across wikis. React or click to claim the one you want.

›
3
w.collection
Build your collection

Your claimed characters are stored. Trade, browse, and flex your collection any time.

Commands

Every command at a glance.

All slash commands have matching prefix equivalents starting with w.

Slash Commands
/roll w.roll Roll 10 random characters from across all enabled wikis.
/collection [@user] w.c [@user] Browse your collection, or anyone else's.
/search <name> w.s <name> Search all collections for a specific character.
/info <name> w.info <name> Get detailed info about a character including wiki source.
/trade @user w.trade @user Initiate a trade offer with another server member.
/remove <name> w.remove <name> Remove a character from your collection.
/wishlist add/addsource w.wl add/src Wishlist a character or an entire Fandom wiki source (3× boost).
/submitimage <name> <url> w.si Submit a custom image for a character card.
/daily w.daily Free daily roll! Build a streak for a bonus claim.
/leaderboard w.lb Top collectors in this server ranked by collection size.
/vote w.vote Vote for WikiRoll on top.gg.
/server w.server Get an invite to the Orbital Outpost community Discord.
/about w.about Bot info, stats, and all links.
/help w.help List all commands grouped by category.
/settings w.settings Configure roll cooldown, claim window, timezone & notify channel. Admin
/source add/remove/list w.source Add or remove Fandom wiki sources for rolling. Admin
/setrollchannel set/clear w.setrc Restrict rolls to a specific channel. Admin
/linkserver start/confirm/status w.linkserver Link two servers to share claimed character ownership. Admin
